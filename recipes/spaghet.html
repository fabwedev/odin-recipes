Spaghet

Description

Delicious spaghet nobody ever gonna touch

What you'll need

  • spaghet
  • pot
  • wo'oh

Steps

  1. Fill pot with wo'oh
  2. Boil pot o' wo'oh
  3. Add spaghet
  4. Let it boil until spaghet is soft
  5. Remove spaghet from wo'oh, add to plate
  6. Add your favourite sauce
  7. Don't let anyone touch yer spaghet
